Job Placement Assistance

We provide personalized job placement assistance to all our students. Our team helps students in preparing resumes, practicing for interviews, and connecting with potential employers.

Internship Opportunities

We offer a wide range of internship opportunities to help students gain practical experience in their fields of study. These internships are with reputable companies and provide valuable learning experiences.

Career Counseling

Our experienced counselors provide one-on-one career counseling sessions to help students explore their interests, strengths, and career goals. We assist students in making informed decisions about their career paths.

Skill Development Workshops

Organizing workshops and seminars on developing essential job skills.Conducting training sessions on communication skills, leadership, and teamwork.

Resource Center

Maintaining an online resource center with job listings, internship opportunities, and career-related articles.
